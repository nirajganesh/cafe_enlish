About Us

HAPPINESS IN A CUP!

Cafenglish was started on March 3, 2000. Currently, we are operating in Raipur, Mumbai and Bangalore. We are the leading brand in the coffee vending machines & instant premix beverages industry. We install semi-automatic and fully automatic vending machines at workplaces in Raipur, Mumbai and Bangalore. Our tea and coffee vending machines plays crucial role in the success of small to large corporates and workplaces. We offer you a wide range of instant premix beverages like coffee, tea, soups and more - all with natural ingredients at your doorstep in right time. Our range of premix drinks are popular among reputed corporates as well as their offices. Significantly few reputed companies are marketing both premix and fresh milk vending machines. Furthermore, we are supplying the coffee vending machine and offering rental services for smaller companies in Raipur, Mumbai & Bangalore locations. We have already installed more than 500 vending machines in these locations.
